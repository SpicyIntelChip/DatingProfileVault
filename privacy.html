Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2025

Overview

Dating Profile Vault is designed with your privacy as a priority. Your dating profile content stays on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All information you enter into the app—including bios, interests, job titles, locations, and prompt answers—is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Data Storage

  • All profile data is stored exclusively on your device
  • Data persists in your device's local storage
  • App settings are stored locally using UserDefaults
  • No cloud sync, no server uploads, no external databases

AI Features

Dating Profile Vault uses Apple Intelligence for AI-powered rewrite suggestions. This processing:

  • Occurs entirely on your device
  • Is handled by Apple's FoundationModels framework
  • Never sends your content to external AI services
  • Requires compatible hardware (iPhone 15 Pro or newer, iPad/Mac with Apple Silicon)

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. When you use the export feature to copy content to dating apps, you manually copy text to your clipboard—no automatic transmission occurs.

Data Deletion

Delete your data at any time by:

  • Removing individual profiles within the app
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all associated data)

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 18 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact
